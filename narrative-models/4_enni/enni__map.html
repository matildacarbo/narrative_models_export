ENNI_Coding schema

Transcription is divided into "communication units, each of which consisted either of (1) one independent clause plus any dependent clauses associated with it, or (2) of a sentence fragment".

Tellings are given a score, rather than coded, same as MAIN (?? is there a purpose-dependent splie like MAIN actually??).
The scoring procedure is described (p. 230-231) + examples shown in Appendix (final page).

expression labelled labelling what labelling how (1): category complexity labelling how (2): categories used
communicative units story grammar units depends on story (and nb of characters in particular); 8 categories or more setting, initiating event, internal response, internal plan, attempt, outcome, character X, reaction of X, (+ duplicates thereof for additional characters)
